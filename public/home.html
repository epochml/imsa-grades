Welcome to IMSA Grades!

Use this tool to find out more about courses across campus. Search across courses, and look below to find overview statistics about many classes. Note this is not an official site. If you find an error, please report it to Cinna Davis (cdavis@imsa.edu).

This site was made for students, parents, teachers and all other community members to have clean and consistent access to grades at the Illinois Mathematics and Science Academy. 2012-2017 data was acquired through a 2017 FOIA request by a Class of 2018 student. Aggregate data from 2018-2021 came from the Office of Institutional Research, and FY22-FY25 grade distributions came from a later FOIA.
