About

This site was made for students, parents, teachers and all other community members to have clean and consistent access to grades at the Illinois Mathematics and Science Academy. 2012-2017 data was acquired through a 2017 FOIA request by a Class of 2018 student. Aggregate data from 2018-2021 came from the Office of Institutional Research. The FY22-FY25 grade distributions come from a later FOIA. The site is currently maintained by Cinna Davis.

Note this is not an official site. If you find an error, please report it.
